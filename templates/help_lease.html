Welcome Students!

Part of our mission here at OurHouse is to help educate students about leases. We understand that for many this is the first time that you will be signing a lease. Below are a few pointers about what to look out for in your lease before you sign it.


Here is a list of topics that you should make sure are touched on in your lease:

  • How long in advance will your landlord alert you that someone will be entering the house?
  • Are sublets allowed? This is important if you intend to go abroad or have someone sublet your room over the summer
  • Who will be responsible for shoveling the sidewalk and driveways? If it's you, who will supply salt and shovels?
  • How long in advance will your landlord alert you that someone will be entering the house?
  • What are the conditions of your security deposit?
  • Will there be a walk through before and after your time in the apartment?
  • Will the apartment be cleaned before you move in?
  • How will rent be collected? One check or seperate? Mailed or picked up?
  • What utilities are included? Gas? Electric? Water? Is it a montly rate? Note: for Boston area students, water should be included.
  • If you wish to use airconditioners, make sure you know exactly how many are allowed.
  • If you intend to get a pet or are moving in with one, know your landlord's stance on them
  • What space is yours? For example, what outdoors space is yours and can you use while you live there? Same goes for the basement
  • What insurance is included? Note: landlord insurance is not the same as renter insurance.
